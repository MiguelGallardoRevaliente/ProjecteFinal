Chamous Lore

Centuries ago, in a magical and enigmatic world known as Chamous, the primordial energy of the universe converged into a unique epicenter: a floating city high above the clouds, also named Chamous. This city, suspended between the skies and the stars, was the core of life and magic that flowed through the vast landscape of Chamous.

In Chamous, the energy of the world, known as the "Radiance," manifested in nine distinct forms, each representing a unique and unparalleled power. These powers, in turn, were personified by nine legendary beings who became the guardians of Chamous. Each guardian was imbued with the essential power of one of the Nine Radiances, and their duty was to preserve the magical balance that sustained the world.

The Nine Radiances, and their respective guardians, were:

  • Awesome, the Radiance of Light: Guardian of light and positive energy.
  • Warlock/Waño, the Radiance of Darkness: Custodian of shadows and dark magic.
  • Chimpy, the Radiance of Wind: Lord of winds and skies.
  • GuRex, the Radiance of Water: Protector of oceans and waters.
  • Tumse, the Radiance of the Forest: Defender of the earth and nature.
  • Maza, the Neutral Radiance: Maintainer of balance and neutrality.
  • Yasai, the Radiance of Poison: Guardian of toxicity and cunning.
  • Rauru, the Radiance of Fire: Master of fire and flames.
  • Morrality, the Radiance of Rock: Custodian of solidity and resilience.

Chamous, despite its apparent peace, witnesses a growing internal conflict. Differences of opinion among the guardians on how to use the Radiance to maintain harmony trigger tensions. While some advocate for the equitable and collaborative use of powers, others crave more control and dominance.

The internal conflict threatens to shatter the delicate balance of Chamous, and players find themselves in the midst of a struggle to preserve the essence of the Nine Radiances and restore fading harmony. Will they be able to overcome internal divisions and protect Chamous from the threat that arises from within?
